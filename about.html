About Page

Welcome to the Non-Profit Charity Webpage!

This is a webpage created by Aleah Crawford, Anthony Dominquez, and Christian Smith in completion of our senior project for the University of Arizona.

This webpage is designed to help you find various non-profit charities with your specfic interest in mind.

How it Works

You can enter a charity type, state, and/or city and we will output a list of non-profit charities that fit your interests.

Once you see a charity that you like just click on it and you will be redirected to the charities specific page. There you can learn imformation about the charity and decide whether or not you would like donate.
